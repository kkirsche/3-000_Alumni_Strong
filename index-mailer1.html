3,000 Alumni Strong
For Hampden-Sydney College

Strength comes from many sources and influences. It is both a state of soundness and vigor, and a reflection of health and vitality. As Hampden-Sydney alumni, our strength comes in our heritage and our deep roots anchored in critical thinking, clear expression, and honor. It was made possible by a dedicated faculty and staff whose influences created our strength of character. To reflect our joint affection for Hampden-Sydney College, we are embarking upon the 3,000 Strong Challenge where we hope that 3,000 alumni will show their belief in the College by making a gift to support our alma mater.
