ABOUT US

Hampden-Sydney College worked tirelessly for our success. The faculty never waivered in their devotion to one thing, helping us reach our goals. For this reason it is our duty to the College to ensure that they can reach their goals. Because without them, we would not have been able to reach ours.

  • 3,000

    ALUMNI STRONG

    No matter what your relationship to the College, we all know what the schoolhas provided. Not only for us, but for others. The College, and it's faculty, staff, and all of it's workers work tirelessly to ensure that we and all others will meet our goals in life through education.

  • OUR

    UNIFIED GOAL

    As they work towards our goals, we too should work for them. Together, we have the opportunity to give back to the College for helping us reach our goals.

    Together, we aim to give 3,000 gifts to the College. Not because we have to, but to show our thanks for everything that has been done, and provided, for us.

  • HOW

    WE CAN DO IT

    This goal can be reached from the comfort of your own home, over the telephone, or by mail.

    Visit Hampden-Sydney's Gifts page to begin the process. Looking to the bottom of the form will show you our phone and mailing address.

  • THE

    BIG REWARD

    By making a gift you will know that you have made a difference in the life of future students.

    Not only have you provided for their future, but you have ensured the College receives a gift that will ensure the faculty like those who have helped you will be able to provide in ways that they were for you.
